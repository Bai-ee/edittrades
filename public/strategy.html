4-Hour Trend Following Strategy

Strategy Philosophy

This is a trend-following, pullback-entry system designed for the 4-hour timeframe. The strategy combines multi-timeframe analysis (4H, 1H, 15M, 5M) with precise entry timing based on EMA structure, Stochastic RSI momentum, and swing-based risk management.

The goal is to catch high-probability moves in the direction of the prevailing 4H trend, entering on pullbacks to the 21 EMA with confirmation from lower timeframes. Risk/Reward: 1.0R to 2.0R.

Entry Rules

LONG Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: UPTREND (SAFE_MODE: must NOT be FLAT; AGGRESSIVE_MODE: can use HTF bias if 4H flat + confidence ≥70%)
  • ✅ 4H Pullback: Price NOT OVEREXTENDED from 21 EMA (gatekeeper: within ±2%)
  • ✅ Entry Zone: ±0.4% buffer around 4H EMA21 (actual entry: 0.996-1.002x EMA21 for LONG)
  • ✅ 1H Alignment: 1H trend NOT breaking down (not DOWNTREND)
  • ✅ 15M & 5M Stoch: NOT both curling down (bullish alignment)
  • ✅ SL: Below 4H swing low (with 0.3% buffer) or 3% below entry
  • ✅ TP: 1.0R (TP1), 2.0R (TP2)
  • ✅ Confidence: 0-100 scale based on trend alignment, stoch, structure, MA confluence

SHORT Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: DOWNTREND (SAFE_MODE: must NOT be FLAT; AGGRESSIVE_MODE: can use HTF bias if 4H flat + confidence ≥70%)
  • ✅ 4H Pullback: Price NOT OVEREXTENDED from 21 EMA (gatekeeper: within ±2%)
  • ✅ Entry Zone: ±0.4% buffer around 4H EMA21 (actual entry: 0.996-1.004x EMA21 for SHORT)
  • ✅ 1H Alignment: 1H trend NOT breaking up (not DOWNTREND)
  • ✅ 15M & 5M Stoch: NOT both curling up (bearish alignment)
  • ✅ SL: Above 4H swing high (with 0.3% buffer) or 3% above entry
  • ✅ TP: 1.0R (TP1), 2.0R (TP2)
  • ✅ Confidence: 0-100 scale based on trend alignment, stoch, structure, MA confluence

Key Filters (NO TRADE if...)

  • ❌ SAFE_MODE: 4H trend is FLAT (hard block - no trade allowed)
  • ❌ AGGRESSIVE_MODE: 4H flat BUT HTF bias confidence <70% or lower TFs not aligned
  • ❌ Price is OVEREXTENDED from 21 EMA (>2% away - gatekeeper check)
  • ❌ 1H trend conflicts with 4H direction (1H breaking down for longs, breaking up for shorts)
  • ❌ 15m/5m stoch both curling against trade direction

Best Practices

Wait for the Setup
Don't force trades. Wait for all timeframe conditions to align.
Respect the 21 EMA
Price should be near it for entries, not far away.
Use Swing Points
Stop loss beyond recent swing high/low gives trade room.
Risk Management
Risk 1% of capital per trade. The edge comes from consistency.
Monitor 4H Closes
Check 4H closes for invalidation signals.
Trust the Process
Not every trade wins. The edge plays out over 50+ trades.

Swing Strategy (3D → 1D → 4H)

Strategy Philosophy

This is a multi-day swing trading system driven by 3-day, 1-day, and 4-hour structure alignment. Swing trades capture larger moves with wider stops at HTF (higher timeframe) levels and targets of 3R, 4R, and 5R.

The goal is to catch major reversals and trend extensions by identifying oversold/overbought pivots on the 3D timeframe and confirming with 1D and 4H momentum. These are multi-day hold trades.

Entry Rules

LONG Swing Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction - hard gatekeeper)
  • ✅ 3D Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction)
  • ✅ 1D Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction)
  • ✅ 3D Pullback: OVEREXTENDED (8-15% below 21 EMA) OR (FLAT + stoch BULLISH/OVERSOLD)
  • ✅ 1D Trend: DOWNTREND BUT with bullish stoch pivot (k < 25)
  • ✅ 1D Pullback: RETRACING or ENTRY_ZONE
  • ✅ 4H Confirmation: UPTREND or (FLAT + stoch BULLISH) + pullback RETRACING/ENTRY_ZONE
  • ✅ Entry Zone: Reclaim level (mid between 1D swing low and 1D EMA21) ±0.5%
  • ✅ SL: Min of 3D/1D swing lows (HTF invalidation)
  • ✅ TP: 3R (TP1), 4R (TP2), 5R (TP3)
  • ✅ Confidence: 70-90% (base 70 + stoch/entry/structure bonuses)

SHORT Swing Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction - hard gatekeeper)
  • ✅ 3D Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction)
  • ✅ 1D Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction)
  • ✅ 3D Pullback: OVEREXTENDED (8-15% above 21 EMA) OR (FLAT + stoch BEARISH/OVERBOUGHT)
  • ✅ 1D Trend: UPTREND BUT with bearish stoch pivot (k > 75)
  • ✅ 1D Pullback: RETRACING or ENTRY_ZONE
  • ✅ 4H Confirmation: DOWNTREND or (FLAT + stoch BEARISH) + pullback RETRACING/ENTRY_ZONE
  • ✅ Entry Zone: Rejection level (mid between 1D swing high and 1D EMA21) ±0.5%
  • ✅ SL: Max of 3D/1D swing highs (HTF invalidation)
  • ✅ TP: 3R (TP1), 4R (TP2), 5R (TP3)
  • ✅ Confidence: 70-90% (base 70 + stoch/entry/structure bonuses)

Key Filters (NO TRADE if...)

  • ❌ 4H trend is FLAT (instant disqualification - hard gatekeeper)
  • ❌ 3D trend is FLAT (must show direction)
  • ❌ 1D trend is FLAT (must show direction)
  • ❌ 3D pullback is not OVEREXTENDED or RETRACING (or FLAT without stoch pivot)
  • ❌ 1D/4H structure not aligned (1D downtrend without bullish pivot for longs, etc.)

Best Practices

HTF Invalidation Only
Use 3D or 1D swing levels for stops, never 4H or lower.
Larger Hold Time
Swing trades take days or weeks. Be patient.
Confidence: 70-90%
Requires strong HTF confluence. Only take A+ setups.
Watch 3D Closes
Monitor 3D closes for invalidation signals.
Wider Stops
HTF swings mean larger SL. Risk 1-2% still applies.
Trail Profits
Consider trailing stop after TP1 to maximize gains.

Scalp Strategy (1H → 15M → 5M)

Strategy Philosophy

This is a lower timeframe momentum scalping system that requires crystal-clear 4H trend support. Scalps are fast in/out trades with tight stops at LTF swing levels and quick targets of 1.5R to 3.0R.

The goal is to catch short-term momentum bursts in the direction of the 4H trend, entering on precise LTF pullbacks to the 21 EMA with tight stoch alignment. Requires active monitoring.

Entry Rules

LONG Scalp Setup

  • ✅ 1H Trend: UPTREND (NOT FLAT - required gatekeeper)
  • ✅ 4H Trend: DISREGARDED (scalp uses 1H bias, ignores 4H)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback: Price near 21 EMA (within ±2% - gatekeeper)
  • ✅ 15M Pullback: Price near 15M EMA21 (within ±1.5%)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback State: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15M Pullback State: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: Aligned with 1H trend (BULLISH/OVERSOLD for longs)
  • ✅ Entry Zone: ±0.4% buffer around 1H EMA21 (0.996-1.004x EMA21 for LONG)
  • ✅ SL: Below 15M/5M swing low (LTF invalidation) with 0.3% buffer
  • ✅ TP: 1.5R (TP1), 3.0R (TP2)
  • ✅ Confidence: 60-85% (base 60 + HTF bias bonus)

SHORT Scalp Setup

  • ✅ 1H Trend: DOWNTREND (NOT FLAT - required gatekeeper)
  • ✅ 4H Trend: DISREGARDED (scalp uses 1H bias, ignores 4H)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback: Price near 21 EMA (within ±2% - gatekeeper)
  • ✅ 15M Pullback: Price near 15M EMA21 (within ±1.5%)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback State: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15M Pullback State: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: Aligned with 1H trend (BEARISH/OVERBOUGHT for shorts)
  • ✅ Entry Zone: ±0.4% buffer around 1H EMA21 (0.998-1.004x EMA21 for SHORT)
  • ✅ SL: Above 15M/5M swing high (LTF invalidation) with 0.3% buffer
  • ✅ TP: 1.5R (TP1), 3.0R (TP2)
  • ✅ Confidence: 60-85% (base 60 + HTF bias bonus)

Key Filters (NO TRADE if...)

  • ❌ 1H trend is FLAT (required gatekeeper - 1H must show direction)
  • ❌ 1H pullback not ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ❌ 15M pullback not ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ❌ Price not near 1H EMA21 (within ±2%) or 15M EMA21 (within ±1.5%)
  • ❌ 15M stoch not aligned with 1H trend direction
  • ⚠️ Note: 4H trend is DISREGARDED for scalps (uses 1H bias only)

Best Practices

HTF Direction Required
Never scalp against 4H trend. If 4H is FLAT, skip scalps.
LTF Invalidation
Use 15M swing levels for stops. Exit quickly if wrong.
Fast Execution
Take profits at 1.5R-3R and move on. Don't overstay.
Active Monitoring
Check every 15-30 minutes. Tighten stops if momentum stalls.
Risk Management
Risk 1% per trade. Tight stops mean more frequent signals.
Time Horizon
Expect movement within 2-8 hours. Not overnight holds.

Micro-Scalp Strategy (Mean Reversion)

⚠️ High-Risk Strategy

This is a mean-reversion countertrend system that operates independently of 4H trend direction. Micro-scalps trade against short-term overextensions on 1H/15M/5M with very tight stops and quick 1.0R-1.5R targets.

WARNING: This is the riskiest strategy. Disregards HTF trend. Use smallest position size (0.25-0.5% risk). Exit immediately if wrong. Available even when 4H is FLAT.

Entry Rules

LONG Micro-Scalp Setup

  • ⚠️ 4H Trend: DISREGARDED - independent of HTF
  • ✅ 1H Trend: UPTREND (not FLAT)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15M Price: Within ±0.25% of 15M EMA21 (SAFE_MODE) or ±0.75% (AGGRESSIVE_MODE)
  • ✅ 5M Price: Within ±0.25% of 5M EMA21 (SAFE_MODE) or ±0.75% (AGGRESSIVE_MODE)
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: OVERSOLD or BULLISH (aligned with 1H uptrend)
  • ✅ 5M Stoch: OVERSOLD or BULLISH (aligned with 1H uptrend)
  • ✅ Entry Zone: Average of 15M/5M EMA21 ±0.4% buffer
  • ✅ SL: Min of 15M/5M swing low (LTF invalidation)
  • ✅ TP: 1.0R (TP1), 1.5R (TP2)
  • ✅ Confidence: 60-75% (base 60 + confluence/stoch bonuses)

SHORT Micro-Scalp Setup

  • ⚠️ 4H Trend: DISREGARDED - independent of HTF
  • ✅ 1H Trend: DOWNTREND (not FLAT)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15M Price: Within ±0.25% of 15M EMA21 (SAFE_MODE) or ±0.75% (AGGRESSIVE_MODE)
  • ✅ 5M Price: Within ±0.25% of 5M EMA21 (SAFE_MODE) or ±0.75% (AGGRESSIVE_MODE)
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: OVERBOUGHT or BEARISH (aligned with 1H downtrend)
  • ✅ 5M Stoch: OVERBOUGHT or BEARISH (aligned with 1H downtrend)
  • ✅ Entry Zone: Average of 15M/5M EMA21 ±0.4% buffer
  • ✅ SL: Max of 15M/5M swing high (LTF invalidation)
  • ✅ TP: 1.0R (TP1), 1.5R (TP2)
  • ✅ Confidence: 60-75% (base 60 + confluence/stoch bonuses)

⚠️ Critical Rules (NO TRADE if...)

  • ⚠️ 4H Trend: DISREGARDED - Micro-scalps ignore HTF direction
  • ❌ 1H trend is FLAT (required gatekeeper - 1H must show direction)
  • ❌ 1H pullback is not ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ❌ Price not within ±0.25% (SAFE) or ±0.75% (AGGRESSIVE) of BOTH 15M and 5M EMA21
  • ❌ Stoch not aligned on both 15M and 5M with 1H trend direction
  • ⚠️ Note: 4H trend is DISREGARDED entirely - micro-scalp is independent of HTF

Best Practices

Smallest Position Size
Risk 0.25-0.5% max. This is the riskiest strategy.
Exit IMMEDIATELY
Don't wait for SL. Exit at first sign of reversal.
Take Profits Fast
Hit TP1 (1.0R) and consider closing. Don't hold.
Disregards 4H Trend
Independent of HTF direction. Focus on 1H/15M/5M only.
Candle-by-Candle
Must be watched closely. Ultra-short timeframes.
Available Anytime
Can trade even when 4H is FLAT. Independent strategy.
