4-Hour "Set & Forget" Strategy

Strategy Philosophy

This is a trend-following, pullback-entry system designed for the 4-hour timeframe on BTCUSDT. The strategy combines multi-timeframe analysis (4h, 1h, 15m, 5m) with precise entry timing based on EMA structure, Stochastic RSI momentum, and swing-based risk management.

The goal is to catch high-probability moves in the direction of the prevailing trend, entering on pullbacks to the 21 EMA with confirmation from multiple timeframes.

Entry Rules

LONG Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: UPTREND (price above 21 & 200 EMA)
  • ✅ 4H Pullback: Price retracing to 21 EMA (not overextended)
  • ✅ 4H Stoch RSI: Oversold or starting to curl up
  • ✅ 1H Trend: Aligned with 4H or at least neutral
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: Showing bullish momentum
  • ✅ 5M Entry: Stoch curl up + close above 21 EMA
  • ✅ SL: Below recent 4H swing low
  • ✅ TP: 1R to 2R (based on SL distance)

SHORT Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: DOWNTREND (price below 21 & 200 EMA)
  • ✅ 4H Pullback: Price retracing to 21 EMA (not overextended)
  • ✅ 4H Stoch RSI: Overbought or starting to curl down
  • ✅ 1H Trend: Aligned with 4H or at least neutral
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: Showing bearish momentum
  • ✅ 5M Entry: Stoch curl down + close below 21 EMA
  • ✅ SL: Above recent 4H swing high
  • ✅ TP: 1R to 2R (based on SL distance)

Key Filters (NO TRADE if...)

  • ❌ 4H trend is FLAT (sideways/choppy market)
  • ❌ Price is OVEREXTENDED from 21 EMA (>3% away)
  • ❌ Stoch RSI conditions not aligned across timeframes
  • ❌ Already in an open position (one trade at a time)

Best Practices

  • 1. Wait for the Setup
    Don't force trades. Wait for all timeframe conditions to align.
  • 2. Respect the 21 EMA
    The 21 EMA on 4H is your trend guide. Price should be near it for entries, not far away.
  • 3. Use Swing Points for SL/TP
    Stop loss goes beyond recent swing high/low. This gives the trade room to breathe.
  • 4. One Trade at a Time
    Don't pyramid or overlap positions. Focus on quality over quantity.
  • 5. Risk Management
    Risk no more than 1-2% of capital per trade. The strategy's edge comes from consistent execution.
  • 6. Trust the Process
    Not every trade will win. Follow the system even after losses. The edge plays out over 50+ trades.
