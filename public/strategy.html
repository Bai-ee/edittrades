4-Hour "Set & Forget" Strategy

Strategy Philosophy

This is a trend-following, pullback-entry system designed for the 4-hour timeframe on BTCUSDT. The strategy combines multi-timeframe analysis (4h, 1h, 15m, 5m) with precise entry timing based on EMA structure, Stochastic RSI momentum, and swing-based risk management.

The goal is to catch high-probability moves in the direction of the prevailing trend, entering on pullbacks to the 21 EMA with confirmation from multiple timeframes.

Entry Rules

LONG Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: UPTREND (price above 21 & 200 EMA)
  • ✅ 4H Pullback: Price retracing to 21 EMA (not overextended)
  • ✅ 4H Stoch RSI: Oversold or starting to curl up
  • ✅ 1H Trend: Aligned with 4H or at least neutral
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: Showing bullish momentum
  • ✅ 5M Entry: Stoch curl up + close above 21 EMA
  • ✅ SL: Below recent 4H swing low
  • ✅ TP: 1R to 2R (based on SL distance)

SHORT Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: DOWNTREND (price below 21 & 200 EMA)
  • ✅ 4H Pullback: Price retracing to 21 EMA (not overextended)
  • ✅ 4H Stoch RSI: Overbought or starting to curl down
  • ✅ 1H Trend: Aligned with 4H or at least neutral
  • ✅ 15M Stoch: Showing bearish momentum
  • ✅ 5M Entry: Stoch curl down + close below 21 EMA
  • ✅ SL: Above recent 4H swing high
  • ✅ TP: 1R to 2R (based on SL distance)

Key Filters (NO TRADE if...)

  • ❌ 4H trend is FLAT (sideways/choppy market)
  • ❌ Price is OVEREXTENDED from 21 EMA (>3% away)
  • ❌ Stoch RSI conditions not aligned across timeframes
  • ❌ Already in an open position (one trade at a time)

Best Practices

  • 1. Wait for the Setup
    Don't force trades. Wait for all timeframe conditions to align.
  • 2. Respect the 21 EMA
    The 21 EMA on 4H is your trend guide. Price should be near it for entries, not far away.
  • 3. Use Swing Points for SL/TP
    Stop loss goes beyond recent swing high/low. This gives the trade room to breathe.
  • 4. One Trade at a Time
    Don't pyramid or overlap positions. Focus on quality over quantity.
  • 5. Risk Management
    Risk no more than 1-2% of capital per trade. The strategy's edge comes from consistent execution.
  • 6. Trust the Process
    Not every trade will win. Follow the system even after losses. The edge plays out over 50+ trades.

Swing Strategy (3D → 1D → 4H)

Strategy Philosophy

This is a multi-day swing trading system driven by 3-day, 1-day, and 4-hour structure alignment. Swing trades capture larger moves with wider stops at HTF (higher timeframe) levels and targets of 3R-5R.

The goal is to catch major reversals and trend extensions by identifying oversold/overbought pivots on the 3D timeframe and confirming with 1D and 4H momentum.

Entry Rules

LONG Swing Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction)
  • ✅ 3D Trend: Bullish or flat-bullish, NOT FLAT
  • ✅ 3D Pullback: OVEREXTENDED (8-15% below 21 EMA)
  • ✅ 3D Stoch: Oversold or bullish pivot
  • ✅ 1D Trend: Downtrend BUT with bullish stoch pivot
  • ✅ 1D Pullback: RETRACING or ENTRY_ZONE
  • ✅ 4H Confirmation: Uptrend or flat-bullish
  • ✅ Entry: Reclaim level (mid between 1D swing low and 1D EMA21) ±0.5%
  • ✅ SL: Min of 3D/1D swing lows
  • ✅ TP: 3R, 4R, 5R

SHORT Swing Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: NOT FLAT (must show direction)
  • ✅ 3D Trend: Bearish or flat-bearish, NOT FLAT
  • ✅ 3D Pullback: OVEREXTENDED (8-15% above 21 EMA)
  • ✅ 3D Stoch: Overbought or bearish rejection
  • ✅ 1D Trend: Uptrend BUT with bearish stoch pivot
  • ✅ 1D Pullback: RETRACING or ENTRY_ZONE
  • ✅ 4H Confirmation: Downtrend or flat-bearish
  • ✅ Entry: Rejection level (mid between 1D swing high and 1D EMA21) ±0.5%
  • ✅ SL: Max of 3D/1D swing highs
  • ✅ TP: 3R, 4R, 5R

Key Filters (NO TRADE if...)

  • ❌ 4H trend is FLAT (instant disqualification)
  • ❌ 3D trend is FLAT
  • ❌ 1D is not trending (must be UPTREND or DOWNTREND)
  • ❌ 3D pullback is not OVEREXTENDED or RETRACING
  • ❌ Price breaks below 1D swing low (long) or above 1D swing high (short)

Best Practices

  • 1. HTF Invalidation Only
    Use 3D or 1D swing levels for stops, never 4H or lower.
  • 2. Larger Position Hold Time
    Swing trades can take days or weeks to play out. Be patient.
  • 3. Confidence: 70-90%
    Swing trades require strong HTF confluence. Only take A+ setups.
  • 4. Watch for 3D Candle Closes
    Monitor 3D closes for invalidation signals.

Scalp Strategy (15m / 5m)

Strategy Philosophy

This is a lower timeframe momentum scalping system that requires crystal-clear HTF trend support. Scalps are fast in/out trades with tight stops at LTF swing levels and quick targets of 1.5R-3R.

The goal is to catch short-term momentum bursts in the direction of the 4H trend, entering on precise LTF pullbacks to the 21 EMA with tight stoch alignment.

Entry Rules

LONG Scalp Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: UPTREND (NOT FLAT - instant block)
  • ✅ 1H Trend: Aligned with 4H
  • ✅ 15m Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE (near 21 EMA)
  • ✅ 15m Stoch: Oversold or bullish curl
  • ✅ 5m Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE (near 21 EMA)
  • ✅ 5m Stoch: Bullish curl/momentum
  • ✅ Entry: Within ±1% of 15m/5m EMA21
  • ✅ SL: Below 5m/15m swing low
  • ✅ TP: 1.5R to 3R

SHORT Scalp Setup

  • ✅ 4H Trend: DOWNTREND (NOT FLAT - instant block)
  • ✅ 1H Trend: Aligned with 4H
  • ✅ 15m Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE (near 21 EMA)
  • ✅ 15m Stoch: Overbought or bearish curl
  • ✅ 5m Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE (near 21 EMA)
  • ✅ 5m Stoch: Bearish curl/momentum
  • ✅ Entry: Within ±1% of 15m/5m EMA21
  • ✅ SL: Above 5m/15m swing high
  • ✅ TP: 1.5R to 3R

Key Filters (NO TRADE if...)

  • ❌ 4H trend is FLAT (golden rule: 4H must show direction)
  • ❌ 1H trend conflicts with 4H
  • ❌ 15m or 5m not in ENTRY_ZONE
  • ❌ Stoch not aligned on both 15m and 5m

Best Practices

  • 1. HTF Direction Required
    Never scalp against 4H trend. If 4H is FLAT, skip scalps entirely.
  • 2. LTF Invalidation
    Use 5m/15m swing levels for tight stops. Exit quickly if wrong.
  • 3. Fast Execution
    Scalps are quick trades. Take profits at 1.5R-3R and move on.
  • 4. Smaller Position Size
    Use smaller size than 4H trades due to tighter stops.

Micro-Scalp Strategy (Mean Reversion)

⚠️ High-Risk Strategy

This is a mean-reversion countertrend system that operates independently of 4H trend direction. Micro-scalps trade against short-term overextensions on 1H/15m/5m with very tight stops and quick 1.0R-1.5R targets.

WARNING: This is the riskiest strategy. Disregards HTF trend. Use smallest position size. Exit immediately if wrong.

Entry Rules

LONG Micro-Scalp Setup

  • ⚠️ 4H Trend: Disregarded - independent of HTF
  • ✅ 1H Trend: UPTREND (not flat)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15m Price: Within ±0.25% of 15m EMA21
  • ✅ 15m Stoch: OVERSOLD or BULLISH
  • ✅ 5m Price: Within ±0.25% of 5m EMA21
  • ✅ 5m Stoch: OVERSOLD or BULLISH
  • ✅ Entry: Average of 15m/5m EMA21
  • ✅ SL: Min of 15m/5m swing low
  • ✅ TP: 1.0R, 1.5R

SHORT Micro-Scalp Setup

  • ⚠️ 4H Trend: Disregarded - independent of HTF
  • ✅ 1H Trend: DOWNTREND (not flat)
  • ✅ 1H Pullback: ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ✅ 15m Price: Within ±0.25% of 15m EMA21
  • ✅ 15m Stoch: OVERBOUGHT or BEARISH
  • ✅ 5m Price: Within ±0.25% of 5m EMA21
  • ✅ 5m Stoch: OVERBOUGHT or BEARISH
  • ✅ Entry: Average of 15m/5m EMA21
  • ✅ SL: Max of 15m/5m swing high
  • ✅ TP: 1.0R, 1.5R

⚠️ Critical Rules (NO TRADE if...)

  • ⚠️ 4H Trend: DISREGARDED - Micro-scalps ignore HTF direction
  • ❌ 1H trend is FLAT (need 1H trending for bias)
  • ❌ 1H pullback is not ENTRY_ZONE or RETRACING
  • ❌ Price not within ±0.25% of BOTH 15m and 5m EMA21
  • ❌ Stoch not aligned on both 15m and 5m
  • ❌ Already in a position (exit micro-scalps FAST)

Best Practices

  • 1. Smallest Position Size
    This is the riskiest strategy. Use 0.25-0.5% risk, not full size.
  • 2. Exit IMMEDIATELY If Wrong
    Don't wait for SL to be hit. Exit at first sign of reversal.
  • 3. Take Profits Fast
    Hit TP1 (1.0R) and consider closing. Don't hold for TP2.
  • 4. Disregards 4H Trend
    This strategy operates independently of HTF direction. Focus on 1H/15m/5m only.
  • 5. Low Win Rate Expected
    Expect 40-50% win rate. This is a high-risk, high-frequency play.
  • 6. Can Trade Anytime
    Available even when 4H trades are blocked by FLAT trend.
